## Research Standards

All research documents in this section adhere to the following standards:

- **Peer-reviewable quality** — Documents are written to academic standards with proper citations and methodology.
- **Reproducibility** — Empirical claims include methodology sufficient for independent reproduction.
- **Intellectual honesty** — Limitations are disclosed, competing approaches are represented fairly, and claims are proportionate to evidence.
- **Currency** — Market data and regulatory references include dates and sources for verification.

## Citation

When citing ACF research, use:
